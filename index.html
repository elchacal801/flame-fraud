About FLAME

FLAME (Fraud Lifecycle Analysis & Mitigation Exchange) is an open-source, community-driven platform for sharing structured fraud detection intelligence.

Each threat path maps fraud schemes across the FS-ISAC Cyber Fraud Prevention Framework (CFPF) five-phase lifecycle, with cross-references to MITRE ATT&CK, Group-IB Fraud Matrix 2.0, and Stripe FT3.
